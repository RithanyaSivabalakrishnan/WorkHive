Access Control

Every role has its own view

WorkHive doesn't give everyone everything — each role sees exactly what they need to act on, nothing more.

🛡️

Admin

Full control within their tenant — users, teams, projects, attendance, and reports.

  • Manage all users & roles
  • Create teams & projects
  • View all attendance
  • Approve leave requests
📋

Manager

Manages their team's tasks, attendance, and queries. Raises issues to admins when needed.

  • Create & assign tasks
  • View team attendance
  • Approve team leave
  • Reply to queries
👤

Employee

Sees their own tasks, clocks in/out, requests leave, and raises queries to their manager.

  • View assigned tasks
  • Clock in / Clock out
  • Request leave
  • Raise queries

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from subscription settings. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle.

Is my data isolated from other tenants?

Completely. Every tenant's data lives in isolated rows scoped by tenant_id. No queries cross tenant boundaries.

What happens when I exceed my user limit?

You'll be prompted to upgrade before new users can be added. Existing users and data are never affected.

What does "Lifetime" mean?

Pay once, use forever. No recurring charges — your plan stays active for the lifetime of the product with all future updates included.

Ready to bring your team together?

Start free. No credit card required. Your data is fully isolated from day one.
